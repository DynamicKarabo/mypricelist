# Partnership Agreement Proposal

## Overview
This document outlines the proposed collaboration terms between myself (Design & Development) and [Partner Name] (Hosting & Referrals). The goal is to establish a mutually beneficial relationship where both businesses grow through reliable service delivery and steady lead generation.

## My Offer (Design & Development)
- **10% Discount on Referred Projects:**
  - Applied to all web projects referred by you.
  - Allows you to margin up or pass savings directly to clients.
  - Maintains high quality at competitive rates.

## What I Need (Hosting & Support)
- **Discounted Hosting:**
  - 15-20% discount on hosting services for clients I refer.
- **Development Environment:**
  - Free test hosting for my personal portfolio and development testing.
- **Referral Process:**
  - **Step 1:** You introduce the client.
  - **Step 2:** I handle the quotation and delivery of the project.
  - **Step 3:** You handle the hosting setup and management.

## Why This Works
- **For You:** Reliable, high-quality design work at better rates for your clients.
- **For Me:** Steady stream of leads and a trusted hosting partner.
- **Synergy:** No one gets squeezed; both businesses grow sustainably.

## Review Terms
- **Performance Review:** If referral volume reaches **3+ projects per month**, we will revisit the discount structure in **3 months** to potentially adjust terms for greater mutual benefit.
